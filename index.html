Georgia🌺

We visited Georgia with kids in 2015, This was our choice for an activity while in Atlanta. We enjoyed the entire experience. We went to a dolphin show and it was fantastic! The shark encounter was great and a real up close view. The bright colors of the Amazon fish are fantastic.
We also visited the gorgeous, elegant BAPS Hindu temple,This place is beyond Beautiful! The detail of the craftsmanship is absolutely stunning, everything is handmade which makes it so much more interesting.
